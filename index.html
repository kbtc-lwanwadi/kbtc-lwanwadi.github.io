About

The Mind Behind
the Vision

I'm Aung Thu, the creative force behind Yankeeverse. Based in Myanmar, I specialize in transforming abstract ideas into striking visual narratives. My work spans across music cover art, brand identity design, merchandise, surreal collage compositions, and cultural illustrations.

From collaborating with Myanmar's hip-hop scene to crafting brand mockups for global aesthetics, every project is a journey into visual alchemy — where art meets purpose, and creativity knows no bounds.
